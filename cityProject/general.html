General Information

Before settling into Hamarschen, there are some things you should know about our town.

  • The city offers beautiful scenery of the lovely Saxon forests!
  • Taxes are relatively high to support the cost of The Witch's Demise festival!
  • Cost of living is relatively low, considering Hamarschen is a medieval town!

We hope you enjoy all Hamarschen has to offer!
